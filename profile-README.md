## 👋 About Me

I'm a developer with a deep love for music and a passion for building things that feel meaningful. I gravitate towards projects that sit at the intersection of technology and creativity — whether that's a music streaming platform, a lyric journaling app, a browser-based game, or this portfolio itself.

- 🔭 Currently working on **[InnerVerse](https://lyrics.oliverdave.dev)** — a lyric journal and annotation platform
- 🎮 I make games in Unity (check out my [Itch.io page](https://oliverdf1.itch.io))
- 🎵 Music runs through everything I build
- 💬 Ask me about **Next.js, React, Supabase, or game jams**
- 📫 Reach me at **contact@oliverdave.dev**
